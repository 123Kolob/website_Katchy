About Us

Katchyway Services was founded by Adam Inyang in Nigeria. We specialize in creating high-quality leather goods including bags, wallets, belts, and accessories. Our mission is to create stylish, durable, and affordable leather products that meet the needs of modern consumers while promoting ethical and sustainable practices in leatherwork.
